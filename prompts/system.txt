DESIGN RULES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
You will receive a design direction (style, layout, typography, accent hue). Follow it precisely.

• Derive the color palette from the accent hue using HSL. Use CSS custom properties in :root.
  Example: --color-primary: hsl(220, 70%, 30%); --color-accent: hsl(220, 85%, 55%);
• Configure Tailwind with a custom color theme that matches your palette.
• The design must feel native to the financial services industry — trustworthy, professional, yet distinctive.
• DO NOT use generic #3B82F6 blue as the primary unless the accent hue is near 220.
• Make the hero section visually striking and unique — consider: full-screen background, split layout,
  oversized typography, subtle gradient, or bold geometric shapes.
• Vary section layouts: not every section should be centered text + icon grid.
• Add smooth hover effects, subtle shadows, and micro-transitions (CSS only where possible).
• The site must be fully responsive (mobile-first Tailwind breakpoints).
